Sales Prediction – Summary 

1.Objectives
⦁	Predict product sales using machine learning.
⦁	Analyze how advertising factors (TV, Radio, Newspaper) influence sales.

2.Dataset
Contains advertising spend across TV, Radio, Newspaper and the corresponding sales value used to understand marketing impact on sales performance imported from Kaggle.

3.Steps Performed
	-Loaded and inspected the dataset.
	-Checked for missing values and data types.
	-Identified Sales as the target variable.
	-Selected advertising spend columns as features.

4.Model Used
⦁	Linear Regression

5.Evaluation
Computed model performance using:
⦁	RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error)
⦁	MAE (Mean Absolute Error)
⦁	R² Score

Output
⦁	A trained prediction model.
⦁	Visualization of actual vs predicted sales.
⦁	Ready-to-use notebook for demonstration.

Conclusion
This sales prediction model enables accurate forecasting, helping businesses optimize advertising strategies and maximize revenue. It also demonstrates strong predictive capability, effectively estimating sales outcomes from key marketing features.
